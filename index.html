Hey there! I’m Lia

Visual Artist & Storyteller

I create fun and meaningful experiences that spark your imagination and warm your heart. Inspired by nostalgia and everyday magic, my work is all about making you feel something special.

To those who love stories,

I'm a third-year Interactive Arts and Technology student at Simon Fraser University. My creative journey is rooted in a deep love for storytelling, sparked by a childhood of crafting and curiosity. Today, that spark fuels my passion for designing interactive projects that connect, inspire, and leave a lasting impression.

I love playing with fun visuals that carries narrative depth. Usually inspired by the quiet beauty of everyday life, I like bringing out that beauty to stir emotion and imagination and believe in the power of small moments creating meaningful impact.

Inside this portfolio, you’ll find a collection of projects that reflect not just what I make, but how I think, from early sketches to final outcomes. Each one tells a story of experimentation, growth, and joy in the process.

Whether you’re here as a collaborator, recruiter, or fellow dreamer, thank you for stopping by.

Warmly,
Lia
